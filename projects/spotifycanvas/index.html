Spotify Canvas

Project inspired by Mitski's song "My Love Mine All Mine." This animation explores enduring love through a figure who embraces it and sends it toward the moon, symbolizing a wish for love to last forever. Using a colored-pencil aesthetic, I gave the piece a warm, human touch. I also designed tattoo illustrations based on the animation, later executed by a tattoo artist.
