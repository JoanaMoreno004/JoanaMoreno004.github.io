Spotify Canvas

Project inspired by Mitski's song "My Love Mine All Mine." This animated piece explores the theme of enduring love, depicting a figure embracing love and launching it towards the moon, symbolizing the desire for this sentiment to last forever. I employed a traditional colored pencil aesthetic to give the animation a warm, human touch. The visual narrative reflects the song's message that love is our only true possession in life
