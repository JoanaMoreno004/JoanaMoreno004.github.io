HiThere! APP

HiThere! is an innovative ideia for a mobile app designed to enhance how friends connect and share memories. With features like a weekly Photo Dump for sharing favorite moments, an integrated shared Calendar with friends for easy event planning, and a dynamic Chat system for personalized communication, HiThere! addresses the need for meaningful digital interactions. The app focuses on creating genuine connections and simplifying social planning while ensuring an engaging user experience. As part of a two-person team, I collaborated closely with @madalenasacarrilho to develop the user experience, from concept to prototype, delivering a seamless and intuitive interface.
