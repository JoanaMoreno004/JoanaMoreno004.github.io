Manifest Zine

Project advocating for the installation of a water fountain at ESAD.CR to ensure access to clean drinking water and reduce plastic bottle waste. I created a manifesto in zine format and a poster to raise awareness about water as a basic right and promote sustainable habits by encouraging reusable bottles. The poster was also designed to be displayed across the university to spread the message further.
