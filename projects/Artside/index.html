ArtSide Event

The ARTSIDE project is a conceptual festival designed to transform Caldas da Rainha into an interactive hub for artistic exploration. Developed in collaboration with two other team members, @mariia_antunes_ and @marsf04, this hypothetical festival targets students and teachers from the Escola Superior de Artes e Design (ESAD) and celebrates the common interest in the arts through immersive experiences. The festival features engaging activities that encourage visitors to explore art in unconventional ways, such as interactive games and sensory journeys. The name "Artside" plays on the idea of venturing into the unknown aspects of art, challenging stereotypes about artistic professions.
